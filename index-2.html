About Ravi Sheth

I am a 2nd year Computer Science, B.S. student currently attending the University of California, San Diego. I am aiming to graduate by June 2020. I started coding my senior year of high school in my A.P. Computer Science class. The code that I wrote in that class mainly focused on mathematical simulations like the birthday problem, and emulating the perfect ecosystem using perdator and prey models. Transitioning to college, I started coding data structures (linked lists, stacks, heaps, tables). By myself I have delved in web development in both front end and back end. I have developed an interest in machine learning hope to focus my energies in that direction in the future.
